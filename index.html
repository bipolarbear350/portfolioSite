Hi, I'm a current student at Brown University, majoring in computer science. I also have a passion for both art and design, particularly at the points where computer science broadens the horizons of what can artists can do.

This page is essentially a continuously updating portfolio where I can share what areas of art I have experience in. I've looked into a wide variety of mediums and styles, and while for the sake of brevity I've simplified the descriptions to the important parts, I'd be happy to elaborate further on any of these projects.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at anthony_ragucci@brown.edu
